Founding story


When I was little, my mother would always prepare a bowl of hot soup and a few simple dishes filled with love for the table. As I grew up and left home, I realized that the most elusive flavor in the world is "the taste of my mother." Therefore, we wanted to open a restaurant not to pursue luxury or sophistication, but to use the most homely and familiar ingredients to create that comforting flavor.
Every dish here has a story:
Scrambled eggs with tomatoes is the quickest way to warm up after school;
Pork rib soup is the product of my mother's patient stew;
Stir-fried rice noodles are the most lively flavor at family gatherings.
We hope that everyone who enters our restaurant will not only be satisfied, but also feel like they are returning home, surrounded by love.

Core Values


Love

Every dish is like a mother's love, prepared with care, not mechanically.



Warmth

Not only the food, but also the restaurant's lighting, The ambiance and service should make people feel at ease, just like a home-cooked dinner table.



Home

Give customers a sense of "home" here, a place where they can unwind and enjoy a peaceful meal.
